Privacy Policy – Knob16

Knob16 does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app processes MIDI data locally on your device for controlling external software and hardware. No MIDI data is transmitted to external servers.

If iCloud sync is enabled, presets and settings may be stored in Apple’s iCloud service and are subject to Apple’s privacy policies.

Knob16 uses Apple services such as StoreKit for in-app purchases. These services are provided by Apple.

No analytics, tracking, or third-party data collection is implemented.
